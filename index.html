Who are we?

The Sub Team Mission

We are a community of driven students from across the University of Washington that believes that everyone is capable of impactful and original work.

Every year, we come together to share our passion with others, and build the fastest human powered submarine in the world.

We compete in the International Submarine Races in Carderock, Maryland, and the European International Submarine Races, in London, England.

The Hull Team

The hull team designs and manufactures all hydrodynamic surfaces, control surfaces, and static geometries of the submarine. Our goal is to create the most hydrodynamic and efficient submarine, while optimizing the stability and control to make a world record two person submarine.

The Electronics Team

Our electronics team programs microcontrollers and small computers to drive the submarine’s control surfaces. We also work on other systems that help the pilots. The hardest challenge we’re taking on is implementing an automated steering system.

The Drivetrain Team

The drivetrain team designs and manufactures a system with the goal of using the pilots’ power to spin the propeller at the ideal rotational velocity in the most reliable, low friction, and space efficient way possible. Our drivetrain uses a series of driveshafts connected to a chain drive by a gearbox to transfer power around the aft pilot to the propeller shaft.

The Propulsion Team

The propulsion team designs and manufactures the tail cone frame, propeller driveshaft, hub, and propellers. Our goal is to achieve a structurally sound and efficient propulsion system that provides thrust for the submarine.

The Dive & Safety Team

The dive and safety team are responsible for the testing of the submarine, and maintaining the safety of the pilots underwater. We also design and manufacture all systems related to the safety of the pilots, namely the dead man switch, pop-up buoy, and hatch release system. We employ a highly trained group of divers, which all have undergone scientific diver training under AAUS.

The Manufacturing Team

Manufacturing team consists of a group of highly motivated and skilled engineers that take the skills learned in the classroom can apply them to a cornucopia of challenging tasks which literally form the backbone of the submarine.
